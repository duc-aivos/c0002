Sourcing & Cultural Significance

At Thịt Chó Gourmet, we honor centuries-old culinary traditions. Our dog meat is sourced from trusted local farms that adhere to strict ethical standards. The meat is processed using time-honored preparation methods to achieve a tender and richly flavored product that resonates with cultural heritage.
